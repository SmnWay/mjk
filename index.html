What We’re About

Michael J. Keating & Associates Inc. is dedicated to helping individuals of all ages live rich and fulfilling lives. Our mission is to provide knowledge, inspiration, and tools for people to take hold of their dreams and reach for the stars if they choose. Operating under several brands, we focus on instilling growth, knowledge, positivity, and kindness. Our brands include:

  • The Quorum for Small Business (Will launch in July 2024)
  • Barnabas & Zachary (coming soon)

The Quorum for Small Business

The Quorum for Small Business focuses on small business owners and those who want to start their own business. We offer classes, seminars, and membership programs to support you, whether you are a one-person enterprise or run a small business with 100 employees.

Learn more in July 2024 (site under construction at https://www.thequorumforsmallbusiness.com/)

Barnabas & Zachary
